## Year 2016, Serie 3

### Year 2016 Serie 3 Problem 1
Consider the following system of linear equations:
\[
\begin{align*}
2x + 4y + 4z &= 4k - 7 \\
2x - ky &= -1 \\
-2x &= k + 1
\end{align*}
\]
1. Discuss the system for different values of the real parameter \( k \).  
   [1 point]
2. Solve the system for \( k = 0 \).  
   [1 point]

### Year 2016 Serie 3 Problem 2
In \( \mathbb{R}^{3} \), consider the line \( r \) with equation \( (x, y, z) = (1 + \lambda, \lambda, 1 - \lambda) \) and the plane \( \pi \) with equation \( 2x - y + z = -2 \).
1. Determine the relative position of the line \( r \) and the plane \( \pi \).  
   [1 point]
2. Calculate the distance between the line \( r \) and the plane \( \pi \).  
   [1 point]

### Year 2016 Serie 3 Problem 3
Consider the function \( f(x) = x e^{x-1} \).
1. Calculate the equation of the tangent line to the graph of the function \( f \) at the point with abscissa \( x = 1 \).  
   [1 point]
2. Determine the intervals where the function \( f \) is increasing and where it is decreasing.  
   [1 point]

### Year 2016 Serie 3 Problem 4
1. Calculate all matrices of the form \( \boldsymbol{A} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ m & -2 \end{pmatrix} \) that satisfy the equality \( \boldsymbol{A}^{2} + \boldsymbol{A} = 2 \boldsymbol{I} \), where \( \boldsymbol{I} \) is the identity matrix, \( \boldsymbol{I} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \).  
   [1 point]
2. Justify that if \( \boldsymbol{A} \) is a square matrix that satisfies the equality \( \boldsymbol{A}^{2} + \boldsymbol{A} = 2 \boldsymbol{I} \), then \( \boldsymbol{A} \) is invertible, and calculate the expression of \( \boldsymbol{A}^{-1} \) in terms of the matrices \( \boldsymbol{A} \) and \( \boldsymbol{I} \).  
   [1 point]

### Year 2016 Serie 3 Problem 5
Consider the tetrahedron with vertices \( A = (x, 0, 1) \), \( B = (0, x, 1) \), \( C = (3, 0, 0) \), and \( D = (0, x, 0) \), with \( 0 < x < 3 \).
1. Verify that the volume of the tetrahedron is given by the expression \( V(x) = \frac{1}{6} (-x^{2} + 3x) \).  
   [1 point]
2. Determine the value of \( x \) that maximizes the volume and calculate this maximum volume.  
   [1 point]

### Year 2016 Serie 3 Problem 6
Consider the parabolas \( f(x) = x^{2} + k^{2} \) and \( g(x) = -x^{2} + 9k^{2} \).
1. Calculate the abscissas, in terms of \( k \), of the intersection points between the two parabolas.  
   [1 point]
2. Calculate the value of the parameter \( k \) so that the area enclosed between the parabolas is 576 square units.

## Year 2016, Serie 5

### Year 2016 Serie 5 Problem 1
Consider the system of linear equations \( \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 & 2 \\ -4 & -1 & -5 \\ 3 & 1 & 4 \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} x \\ y \\ z \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} b_{1} \\ b_{2} \\ b_{3} \end{pmatrix} \).
1. Explain whether the following statements are true or false:
   - If \( \begin{pmatrix} b_{1} \\ b_{2} \\ b_{3} \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix} \), the system is compatible and determined, and the solution is \( \begin{pmatrix} x \\ y \\ z \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix} \).  
     [1 point]
   - If \( \begin{pmatrix} b_{1} \\ b_{2} \\ b_{3} \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix} \), the system is compatible and indeterminate.  
     [1 point]

### Year 2016 Serie 5 Problem 2
In \( \mathbb{R}^{3} \), consider the plane \( \pi \) with equation \( x - y + 2z = 2 \) and the points \( A = (3, -1, 2) \) and \( B = (1, 1, -2) \).
1. Verify that the points \( A \) and \( B \) are symmetric with respect to the plane \( \pi \).  
   [1 point]
2. If \( r \) is the line of points \( P \) with equation \( P = B + \lambda v \), where \( \lambda \) is a real parameter and \( v = (1, 1, 0) \), verify that the midpoints of the segments \( \overline{AP} \) belong to the plane \( \pi \).  
   [1 point]

### Year 2016 Serie 5 Problem 3
1. Calculate the relative maxima, minima, and inflection points of the function \( f(x) = 2x^{3} - 9x^{2} + 12x - 4 \).  
   [1 point]
2. Explain that if \( f(x) \) is a function with a continuous first derivative in the interval \( [a, b] \) and satisfies \( f'(a) > 0 \) and \( f'(b) < 0 \), then there is at least one point in the interval \( (a, b) \) where the tangent line to the graph of \( f(x) \) at that point is horizontal.  
   [1 point]

### Year 2016 Serie 5 Problem 4
Let \( \boldsymbol{A} \) be a square matrix of order \( n \) that satisfies the equality \( \boldsymbol{A} \cdot (\boldsymbol{A} - \boldsymbol{I}) = \boldsymbol{I} \), where \( \boldsymbol{I} \) is the identity matrix.
1. Justify that the matrix \( \boldsymbol{A} \) is invertible and that \( \boldsymbol{A}^{-1} = \boldsymbol{A} - \boldsymbol{I} \).  
   [1 point]
2. Calculate the value of \( a \) that makes the matrix \( \boldsymbol{A} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & a \end{pmatrix} \) satisfy the equality \( \boldsymbol{A} \cdot (\boldsymbol{A} - \boldsymbol{I}) = \boldsymbol{I} \). Calculate \( \boldsymbol{A}^{-1} \) and verify that it corresponds to the matrix calculated from the result of the previous part.  
   [1 point]

### Year 2016 Serie 5 Problem 5
