## Year 2022, Serie 2

### Problem 1
Let \( f'(x) = 3x^{2} - 12x \) be the derivative of a function \( f(x) \).
1. If we know that \( f(x) \) intersects the x-axis at \( x = 1 \), calculate the expression of the function \( f(x) \).  
   [0.75 points]
2. Calculate the abscissa of the inflection point of \( f(x) \) and study the concavity of the function.  
   [0.75 points]
3. We know that the area of the region bounded by the curve \( y = f''(x) \), the x-axis, and the lines \( x = 0 \) and \( x = a \), with \( a > 2 \), is \( 15 \, \text{u}^{2} \). Calculate the value of \( a \).  
   [1 point]

### Problem 2
Consider the following system of linear equations, which depends on the real parameter \( a \):
\[
\begin{align*}
a x + 2y + 3z &= 2 \\
2x + ay + z &= a \\
x + y + 4z &= 1
\end{align*}
\]
1. Discuss the system for different values of the parameter \( a \).  
   [1.5 points]
2. Solve the system, if possible, for the case \( a = 2 \).  
   [1 point]

### Problem 3
Let the line \( r \) be defined by the following expression:
\[
r: \begin{cases}
x = 2 + \lambda \\
y = -1 + 3\lambda \\
z = 3 + \lambda
\end{cases}
\]
1. Determine the relative position of the line \( r \) with respect to the plane \( \pi: x - 2y + 4z - 4 = 0 \). If it is parallel, calculate the distance from \( r \) to \( \pi \), and if it is secant, calculate the intersection point.  
   [1.25 points]
2. Calculate the equation of the line \( s \) perpendicular to the plane \( \pi \) and intersecting the line \( r \) at a point \( P \), whose first coordinate is 5 times larger than the second.  
   [1.25 points]

### Problem 4
1. Find a polynomial function \( y = g(x) \) of degree 3 such that it intersects the y-axis at the point \( (0, 5) \), the tangent line to \( y = g(x) \) at the point with abscissa \( x = 1 \) is horizontal, and \( g''(x) = 2x + 1 \).  
   [1 point]
2. Verify that the function \( f(x) = -x^{3} + 6x^{2} - 16 \) has a root at \( x = 2 \) and is strictly increasing in the interval \( (0, 4) \). Use this information to calculate the area determined by the function \( f(x) \), the x-axis, and the lines \( x = 0 \) and \( x = 4 \).  
   [1.5 points]

### Problem 5
Let the matrix \( \boldsymbol{X} = \begin{pmatrix} a & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & b & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & c \end{pmatrix} \), which depends on the parameters \( a, b, \) and \( c \).
1. Calculate the matrices \( \boldsymbol{X} \) such that \( \boldsymbol{X}^{2} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \).  
   [1 point]
2. Determine the values of \( a, b, \) and \( c \) so that the inverse matrix of \( \boldsymbol{X} \) is \( \boldsymbol{X}^{-1} = \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1}{2} & -\frac{1}{2} & -\frac{1}{2} \\ 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & -1 \end{pmatrix} \).  
   [1 point]

### Problem 6

## Year 2022, Serie 5

### Problem 1
Let the matrices \( \boldsymbol{C} = \begin{pmatrix} -1 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{pmatrix} \) and \( \boldsymbol{A} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 0 \\ 3 & -1 \end{pmatrix} \).
1. Verify that \( \boldsymbol{C}^{3} = \boldsymbol{I}_{2} \), where \( \boldsymbol{I}_{2} \) is the identity matrix of order 2, and deduce that the matrix \( \boldsymbol{C} \) is invertible and that \( \boldsymbol{C}^{-1} = \boldsymbol{C}^{2} \). Calculate \( \boldsymbol{C}^{2022} \).  
   [1.5 points]
2. Solve the matrix equation \( \boldsymbol{C} \cdot \boldsymbol{X} = \boldsymbol{A} - 2 \boldsymbol{I}_{2} \).  
   [1 point]

### Problem 2
Consider the function \( f(x) = x^{3} \) and let \( a \) be a strictly positive real number.
1. Calculate the equation of the line \( t \) tangent to the graph of the function \( f \) at the point with abscissa \( x = a \). Find the intersection point of the line \( t \) with the x-axis (in terms of \( a \)).  
   [1.25 points]
2. Sketch the graph of the function \( f \) and the line \( t \). Calculate the value of \( a \) so that the area in the first quadrant bounded by the function \( f \), the line \( t \), and the x-axis is \( 108 \, \text{u}^{2} \).  
   [1.25 points]

### Problem 3
Consider the system of linear equations
\[
\begin{align*}
2x - y + 3z &= 0 \\
my + (3 - m)z &= -6 \\
2x - y + mz &= 6
\end{align*}
\]
where \( m \) is a real parameter.
1. Discuss the system for different values of the parameter \( m \).  
   [1.25 points]
2. Solve the system, if possible, when \( m = 0 \) and when \( m = 3 \). In each case, give the relative position of the three planes in \( \mathbb{R}^{3} \).  
   [1.25 points]

### Problem 4

1. Express the area of the triangle \( AOB \) as a function of \( x \). For what value of \( x \) is the area of the triangle \( AOB \) the largest possible? What is this maximum area?  
   [1.25 points]
2. Express the hypotenuse of the triangle \( AOB \) as a function of \( x \). For what value of \( x \) is the hypotenuse of the triangle \( AOB \) the smallest possible? What is this minimum value?  
   [1.25 points]
