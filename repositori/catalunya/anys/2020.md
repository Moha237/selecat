## Year 2020, Serie 1

### Problem 1
We draw the tangent line to the function \( f(x) = \frac{1}{x^{2}} + 1 \) at a point \( P = (a, f(a)) \) in the first quadrant. This line, together with the coordinate axes, forms a triangle.
1. Verify that the area of this triangle, as a function of \( a \), is given by the function
   \[
   g(a) = \frac{(a^{2} + 3)^{2}}{4a}
   \]
   [1.25 points]
2. At which point \( P \) is the area of the triangle minimal? Calculate this minimum value.  
   [1.25 points]

### Problem 2
Consider the following system of linear equations, which depends on the real parameter \( k \):
\[
\begin{align*}
5x + y + 4z &= 19 \\
kx + 2y + 8z &= 28 \\
5x + y - kz &= 23 + k
\end{align*}
\]
1. Discuss the system for different values of the parameter \( k \).  
   [1.25 points]
2. Solve the system, if possible, for the case \( k = 0 \).  
   [1.25 points]

### Problem 3
1. Calculate the general equation of the plane \( \pi \) that passes through the point \( (8, 8, 8) \) and has direction vectors \( \boldsymbol{u} = (1, 2, -3) \) and \( \boldsymbol{v} = (-1, 0, 3) \).  
   [1.25 points]
2. Determine the value of the parameter \( a \) so that the point \( (1, -5, a) \) belongs to the plane \( \pi \) and calculate the parametric equation of the line that passes through this point and is perpendicular to the plane \( \pi \).  
   [1.25 points]

### Problem 4
Consider the function \( f(x) = \frac{ax^{2} + b}{x} \), where \( a \) and \( b \) are two real parameters. Calculate the values of \( a \) and \( b \) so that the function \( f(x) \) has an oblique asymptote with slope 1 and a minimum at the point on the graph with abscissa \( x = 2 \).  
   [2.5 points]

### Problem 5
Let the matrix \( \boldsymbol{A} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -3 & -4 \end{pmatrix} \).
1. Find the matrix \( \boldsymbol{X} \) that satisfies the equation \( \boldsymbol{A X} = \boldsymbol{I} - 3 \boldsymbol{X} \), where \( \boldsymbol{I} \) is the identity matrix of order 2.  
   [1.25 points]
2. Verify that the matrix \( \boldsymbol{X} \) is invertible and calculate its inverse matrix.  
   [1.25 points]

### Problem 6
Consider the function \( f(x) = x^{3} \).
1. Calculate at which point in the third quadrant the tangent line to \( y = f(x) \) is parallel to the line \( 3x - y = 4 \). Calculate the equation of the tangent line to the graph at this point and make an approximate sketch of the graph of the function and the two lines.  
   [1.25 points]
2. Calculate the area of the region bounded by \( y = f(x) \) and the line \( y = 3x + 2 \).  
   [1.25 points]

### Problem 1

### Problem 2

There is a path that partially surrounds the cave formed by the curve arc \( y = 4 - x^{2} \) with endpoints \( (0, 4) \) and \( (2, 0) \). The cave is located at the point with coordinates \( (0, 2) \), as shown in the figure, and a straight access \( d \) from the path to the cave is to be enabled, which is as short as possible.
1. Identify on the graph in the figure the coordinates of the cave and the point on the path from which access is to be enabled. Verify that the function \( f(x) = \sqrt{x^{4} - 3x^{2} + 4} \) calculates the distance from each point on the path to the cave.

2. Calculate the coordinates of the point on the path that is closest to the cave and state what the length of the access \( d \) will be.  
   [1.25 points]

### Problem 4
