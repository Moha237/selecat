## Year 2019, Serie 1

### Problem 1
The pages of a book must each have an area of \( 600 \, \text{cm}^{2} \), with margins around the text of 2 cm at the bottom, 3 cm at the top, and 2 cm on each side. Calculate the dimensions of the page that allow for the largest possible printed area.  
   [2 points]

### Problem 2
Consider the following system of linear equations, which depends on the real parameter \( k \):
\[
\begin{align*}
x + 3y + 2z &= -1 \\
x + k^{2}y + 3z &= 2k \\
3x + 7y + 7z &= k - 3
\end{align*}
\]
1. Discuss the system for different values of the parameter \( k \).  
   [1 point]
2. Solve the system for the case \( k = -1 \).  
   [1 point]

### Problem 3
A drone is located at the point \( P = (2, -3, 1) \) and we want to direct it in a straight line to the closest point on the plane with equation \( \pi: 3x + 4z + 15 = 0 \).
1. Calculate the parametric equation of the line that the drone must follow. What distance must it travel to reach the plane?  
   [1 point]
2. Find the coordinates of the point on the plane where the drone will arrive.  
   [1 point]

### Problem 4
Consider the function \( f(x) = \frac{2x^{3} - 5x + 4}{1 - x} \).
1. Calculate its domain and study its continuity. Does it have any vertical asymptotes?  
   [1 point]
2. Observe that \( f(-2) = -\frac{2}{3}, f(0) = 4 \), and \( f(2) = -10 \). Reason whether, based on this information, we can deduce that the interval \( (-2, 0) \) contains a zero of the function. Can we deduce it for the interval \( (0, 2) \)? Find an interval determined by two consecutive integers that contains at least one zero of this function.  
   [1 point]

### Problem 5
Let the matrix \( \boldsymbol{M} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & a \\ a & 0 \end{pmatrix} \), where \( a \) is a real parameter.
1. Calculate for which values of the parameter \( a \) the equality \( \boldsymbol{M}^{2} - \boldsymbol{M} - 2 \boldsymbol{I} = \mathbf{0} \) is satisfied, where \( \boldsymbol{I} \) is the identity matrix and \( \mathbf{0} \) is the null matrix, both of order 2.  
   [1 point]
2. Using the equality from the previous part, find a general expression to calculate the inverse matrix of \( \boldsymbol{M} \) and then calculate the inverse of \( \boldsymbol{M} \) for the case \( a = \sqrt{2} \).  
   [1 point]

### Problem 6
Consider the functions \( f(x) = x^{2} \) and \( g(x) = \frac{1}{x} \), and the line \( x = \mathrm{e} \).
1. Make a sketch of the region bounded by their graphs and the x-axis. Calculate the coordinates of the intersection point of \( y = f(x) \) with \( y = g(x) \).  
   [1 point]
2. Calculate the area of the region described in the previous part.  
   [1 point]

## Year 2019, Serie 4

### Problem 1
We want to build a rectangular wooden frame that encloses an area of \( 2 \, \text{m}^{2} \). We know that the price of the wood is \( 7.5 \, € / \text{m} \) for the horizontal sides and \( 12.5 \, € / \text{m} \) for the vertical sides. Determine the dimensions that the rectangle must have so that the total cost of the frame is as low as possible. What is this minimum cost?  
   [2 points]

### Problem 2
Let the line \( r: \begin{cases} x = 2 \\ y - z = 1 \end{cases} \) and the plane \( \pi: x - z = 3 \).
1. Calculate the parametric equation of the line that is perpendicular to the plane \( \pi \) and intersects it at the same point where the line \( r \) intersects it.  
   [1 point]
2. Find the points on \( r \) that are at a distance of \( \sqrt{8} \) units from the plane \( \pi \).  
   [1 point]

### Problem 3
Consider the following system of linear equations, which depends on the real parameter \( a \):
\[
\begin{align*}
ax + 7y + 5z &= 0 \\
x + ay + z &= 3 \\
y + z &= -2
\end{align*}
\]
1. Discuss the system for different values of the parameter \( a \).  
   [1 point]
2. Solve the system for the case \( a = 2 \).  
   [1 point]

\end{cases}
